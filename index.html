About me

Hi, I'm Mateo Suffern, a Master's student in Mathematical-Computational Modeling and Data Science at the University of Buenos Aires. My main research interest is geometry, supported by coursework in continuous modelling, complex networks, and topological data analysis and an ongoing project in network growth and self-similarity. Currently, I'm preparing my Master's thesis on geometrical complexity applied to fluid dynamics. I'm also part of the ArtGAN Group at the Institute of Computer Science (UBA), where I work on synthetic facial expression generation, supervised by Professor Pablo Negri.
